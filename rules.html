SnapShortz RULES & GUIDELINES

Welcome to SnapShortz. These rules are designed to keep the platform safe, respectful, and enjoyable for everyone. Violating these rules may result in warnings, suspension, or permanent account termination.

1. Spam & Unwanted Content

Posting repetitive, misleading, or unwanted content such as spam links, fake promotions, or mass messaging is strictly prohibited.

2. False Information & Fraud

Sharing fake news, misinformation, or engaging in scams and fraudulent activities will lead to immediate action, including permanent bans.

3. Impersonation

You may not impersonate another person, brand, or organization. Accounts found doing so will be removed without notice.

4. Artificial Engagement

Follow-for-follow schemes, fake likes, bots, or any attempt to artificially boost engagement is not allowed.

5. AI-Generated Content

AI-generated content must be clearly labeled, especially when it represents real people, events, or news.

6. Sensitive / 18+ Content

Any sensitive or adult content must be properly marked as 18+. Failure to do so may result in content removal or account penalties.

7. Abuse & Harmful Behavior

  • Harassment or bullying
  • Hate speech
  • Threats or intimidation

These behaviors are strictly prohibited. However, respectful discussions and debates are allowed.

8. Enforcement

SnapShortz reserves the right to review, remove content, and take action against accounts that violate these rules at any time.

9. Updates to Rules

These guidelines may be updated at any time. Continued use of the platform means you accept any changes.
